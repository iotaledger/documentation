# Why build Channels applications

**Channels comes with lots of features for securely communicating with other devices and authenticating the owner of the messaging channel. This article discusses the reasons for building your own applications on Channels.**

## Secure your communications

Messages can be signed and encrypted, allowing you to build applications that authenticate the sender and restrict access to private messages.

## Ensure the integrity of messages

Messages can be published on the Tangle, making them transparent and tamperproof.

## Innovate on bleeding edge technology

Messages use quantum-robust cryptography as well as distributed ledger technology, putting your applications at the forefront of innovation in these fields.
